Traffic Dataviz

A vizualization of a morning commute from January to August 2015 in the OKC metro area. GPS data was logged on an Android device, parsed with Python, and vizualized using Cesium.js and a CartoDB basemap.

Three.js Heart Viz

Developed for a college anatomy class, this Three.js visualizes a model of a human heart. In the anatomy class, students are required to identify components of the heart using the heart model, but never before had access to the model outside of class for study. With this heart visualization, students can study for the anatomy exam, even on a smartphone while they're on the bus. This digital version of the physical model was scanned using hundreds of photos, and digitized using Agisoft Photoscan. Three.js provides an easy way to render the .obj and .mtl files that Photoscan exports. Please note this is still in development, and you must click and hold your left mouse button and move the mouse to make the heart appear initially.

Hike up Elk Mountain

A virtual trip up Elk Mountain in Oklahoma, using HTML5, GIS files, Cesium.js, and Panellum.js. The user can visualize the path up the mountain, its relationship surrounding mixed grass prairie, and sample the flora and fauna (including the state reptile).
